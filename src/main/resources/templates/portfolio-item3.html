You Should Try These Korean Food!

Korean Food:
Korean Cuisine is mainly relied on grains, vegetables and meats. There are various side dishes are involved in Korean food as well. The most special and exotic side dish is Kimchi, which is made from salted and fermented vegetables that almost always served with every kinds of Korean meals. Kimchi is famous for the appetite like pickles that contains amounts of good nutritious such as vitamin C, and fiber.

Korean Meats:
The meat dishes are also favoured by Koreans and Westerners. The Bulgogi literally means the fire meat that is marinated slices of beef or pork with a mixture of soy sauce, garlic, sugar, sesame oil, and other condiments, often cooked over a fire in front of table or on a stove - top griddle.

Korean Vegetables:
Korean cuisines use a different variety of veggies, which is often cooked in various stews, stir-fried dishes, and other hot dishes. The most common vegetables for Korean dish include Korean radish, napa cabbage, cucumber, potato, sweet potato, spinach, bean sprouts, scallions, garlic, chili peppers, seaweed, zucchini, mushrooms and lotus root. There types of vegetables are all popular dishes among Koreans, hence it is highly recommended for people to taste them.
